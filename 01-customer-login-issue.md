# Case 01: Customer Login Issue
**Date:** 24 Dec 2025  
**Platform/Tool:** Example SaaS App  

## Issue Summary
A customer reported being unable to log into their account despite using the correct credentials. The login page repeatedly returned an error message stating "Invalid credentials."

## Steps Taken
1. Verified the customerâ€™s account status in the admin panel; account was active and not locked.  
2. Confirmed that the customer was entering the correct username and password.  
3. Guided the customer through clearing browser cache and cookies.  
4. Instructed the customer to attempt login from an incognito/private browsing window.  
5. When the issue persisted, generated a password reset link and confirmed the customer successfully received it.  
6. Followed up with the customer to ensure login was successful.  

## Outcome/Resolution
The customer successfully logged in after resetting their password. Provided additional guidance on saving credentials and ensuring browser cookies are enabled for future logins. Customer expressed satisfaction with the support received.
